Various Subsidiy for Women

Generation-Based Incentives (GBIs)

The government offers GBIs for grid-connected renewable energy projects such as solar, wind, and biomass. These incentives provide financial support based on the actual electricity generated by the project.

Capacity Building and Mentorship

The government may offer capacity building programs and mentorship opportunities for women in the renewable energy sector. These initiatives provide guidance, mentorship, and networking support to help women entrepreneurs and professionals succeed in the industry. The government provides various subsidies to NGOs led by women.

Solar Rooftop Subsidy

The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) provides subsidies and financial support for installing solar rooftop systems through various state-specific schemes.
These subsidies are available to both residential and commercial consumers
